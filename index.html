Hello, thanks for visiting my personal webpage! I am a computer science student about to begin my second year at the University of Cambridge (Robinson College).

I am primarily interested in Machine Learning because I love how simple, mathematical rules can create complex, intelligent systems with a deeper, fundamental understanding of a problem than what would be possible by using explicit heuristics. I think ML has amazing practical applications for many disciplines because it allows us to deploy adaptable, cutting edge technology on a massive scale.

I am also interested in security because I think it is really exciting to take a product and, rather than asking "what does this do?", ask "what *can* this do?". I think, as Machine Learning is used more and more in every day life, the role of security in ML will become increasingly relevant in protecting our privacy.
